Getting started

  • What is ForexPulse?

    ForexPulse is a trading platform for Forex and prop trading. You get real-time rates, interactive charts (TradingView), currency conversion, a personal watchlist, market session times, performance reports, and a dashboard—all in one place. No paid plans; sign in to save your preferences and watchlist.

  • Do I need an account?

    You can browse Home, All Pairs, Tools (converter, pip value, etc.), and Market without signing in. Sign in to use Dashboard (balance chart, loss analysis), Watchlist (saved pairs), Performance, and Settings (display name, password).

  • How do I create an account?

    Click Sign in in the sidebar or top bar, then open the Register tab under User. Enter your name, email, and password (min 6 characters) and submit. You can then sign in from any page.

  • How do I sign in as admin?

    On the Sign in page, select the Admin tab and enter your admin credentials. You will be redirected to the admin area (user management, etc.) after login.

Platform & pages

A short guide to every main section of the app. Use the sidebar or the Quick links above to open any page.

  • Home — Overview with a featured pair chart, portfolio snapshot, converter, trending pairs, categories, and trading sessions.
  • All Pairs — Full list of forex pairs with live rates. Click a pair to open its detail page with chart and converter.
  • Dashboard — Account balance chart, loss analysis, quick links, and (in Trading Overview) P/L, metrics, and trade history.
  • Watchlist — Your saved pairs in one table with live rates. Add pairs from All Pairs or from a pair detail page; remove from the Watchlist table.
  • Market — Trading session times (Sydney, Tokyo, London, New York) in UTC, with Open/Closed status and session overlap info.
  • Performance — Period-based reports (Week / Month / Quarter): total P&L, win rate, trades, best day, and daily summary table.
  • Tools — Pip value, position size, currency converter, and price alerts. Pick a tool from the grid to use it.
  • Settings — Change your display name and password. Available when signed in.

Trading & dashboard

  • Where can I see live rates and charts?

    Home shows a featured pair and trending list. All Pairs lists every pair with rates; click a pair for its Pair detail page (full chart + converter). Dashboard shows your account balance chart. Charts use TradingView and support timeframes (e.g. 1h, 4h, 1d, 1w) and fullscreen.

  • What does the Dashboard show?

    Two tabs: Account Overview — balance chart, account loss analysis (initial deposit / daily loss level), and quick links. Trading Overview — P/L card, trading objectives (metrics grid), and trading history table with search.

  • How do I convert currencies?

    Go to Tools and open Currency converter. Enter the amount and choose “from” and “to” currencies; the result updates using current rates. You can also use the converter on Home.

  • Where do exchange rates come from?

    Rates are supplied by the Frankfurter API (external). Charts are powered by TradingView. The platform does not execute trades; it is for analysis and tools.

Watchlist, Market & Performance

  • What is the Watchlist?

    The Watchlist page shows pairs you’ve saved, with live rates and a “Change” column. Use Add from All Pairs to open All Pairs and add more, or from a Pair detail page use Add to watchlist. Remove a pair with the Remove button in the table. Your list is stored in your browser.

  • What is the Market page?

    The Market page shows the four main forex sessions (Sydney, Tokyo, London, New York) with open/close times in UTC. Each session has an Open or Closed status based on current time. A separate card explains session overlaps (e.g. London/NY) when volatility is often higher.

  • What is the Performance page?

    The Performance page shows period-based reports. Choose Week, Month, or Quarter. You’ll see total P&L, win rate, number of trades, best day, and a daily summary table. Data is illustrative; connect your own data source for live brokerage stats.

Tools

  • What tools are available?

    Pip value — Calculate pip value in USD for a pair and lot size. Position size — Compute lot size from balance, risk %, and stop loss. Currency converter — Convert amounts between currencies with live rates. Price alerts — Manage price alerts for pairs (e.g. above/below a level). Open Tools and click a card to use one.

  • How do I set a price alert?

    Go to Tools → Price alerts. Add a new alert by choosing the pair, condition (e.g. above/below), and price. Alerts are stored in your browser. Note: the platform does not send push or email notifications; you need to check the alerts list or integrate with a notification service separately.

Account & support

  • I forgot my password. What do I do?

    Go to the Sign in page and click Forgot password? below the Sign in button. Enter your account email and click Continue. If an account exists, you can set a new password (min 6 characters), confirm it, then click Reset password. Sign in with the new password afterward.

  • How do I change my display name?

    Sign in and open Settings. Under Account, edit Display name and click Save name. This name appears in the top bar and on the dashboard account header.

  • How do I open or close the sidebar?

    Click the chevron button at the top of the sidebar (double-arrow icon). The sidebar expands or collapses; the state is saved in your browser and kept when you move between pages (Home, Dashboard, Watchlist, Market, Performance, Tools, etc.).

  • Is there a search for pairs?

    Yes. Use the Search button in the top bar or in the sidebar. A search modal opens: type to find pairs by name or symbol, then click a result to go to that pair’s detail page.

  • Where can I get more help?

    You’re on the Help Center. Use the Jump to section links above to find topics. For account or broker-specific issues, contact your platform administrator or refer to your broker’s documentation. Trading carries risk; past performance is not indicative of future results.

Help Center

ForexPulse is a trading platform for forex and prop trading. You can view real-time rates, charts, convert currencies, and manage your dashboard from one place.

Go to Home or All Pairs. You’ll see live rates for major and minor pairs. Click a pair to open its detail page with the full chart and more data.

The converter is on the Tools page, along with pip value, position size, and price alerts. You can also use the converter on Home.

You can view pairs, charts, and use the converter without signing in. Sign in to see your portfolio snapshot, balance, and save your preferences on the dashboard.
